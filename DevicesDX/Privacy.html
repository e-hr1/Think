Privacy Policy

For Devices DX

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

At "Devices DX," we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our application. By using "Devices DX," you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

The "Devices DX" application is designed as a utility tool that does not require the collection of personally identifiable information.

  • Information We Do Not Collect: We do not collect or store any direct personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or geographic location. The app does not require you to create an account.
  • Service Usage Information: When you use the "Error Code Analysis" feature, the information you enter (error code, device name, manufacturer, and model) is sent to a third-party API, Google Gemini, to process your request and provide a diagnosis. We do not store these queries on our servers.
  • Log Data: In the event of a software error within the app, we may collect non-personal data to help diagnose and fix the problem. This data may include your device type, operating system version, and the time of the error.

2. How We Use Information

The data processed through the app is used for the following purposes only:

  • To Provide the Core Service: The error code data you provide is used to process your request via the Google Gemini API and deliver the analysis results to you.
  • To Improve the Application: We may use non-personal log data to understand technical issues and improve the app's stability and performance.

3. Information Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party, as we do not collect it in the first place.

  • Service Providers (Google Gemini API): Error analysis queries are shared with Google for processing. This data is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We advise you to review Google's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle data.
  • External Links: The app may contain links to external services (like WhatsApp and Instagram). If you click on these links, you will be directed to their sites. We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information processed through our app. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Children's Privacy

Our application is intended for professionals in the biomedical engineering field and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any updates.
